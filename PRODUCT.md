# Product

## Register

product: an google extension side panel

## Users
Social media researchers and data analysts looking to extract structured data from platforms like TikTok, Shopee, Facebook, and Instagram.

## Product Purpose
To provide a fast, reliable, and lightweight browser utility to export reviews, comments, and sort profiles/feeds. Success looks like error-free data extraction, zero workflow disruption, and clear visual feedback during long-running background tasks.

## Brand Personality
* Minimalist
* Clean
* Expert
* High-Contrast

The emotional goal is to inspire confidence, efficiency, and clarity.

## Anti-references
* Saturated SaaS warm-cream dashboards with unnecessary decorative badges.
* Excessive bounce animations or layout shifting.
* Over-rounded buttons (> 16px border-radius) or ghost-cards with wide blurry drop shadows.

## Design Principles
1. **Utility First**: The design should never get in the way of user workflows. Data export controls and progress bars must be prominent and instantly understandable.
2. **Deterministic Feedback**: Long scraping operations should have highly predictable progress states, clear success/failure indicators, and smooth micro-animations.
3. **Harmonious Contrast**: Maintain crisp readable text (meet accessibility standards) with color-coded accents that map directly to the platform being targeted (e.g., green/blue/orange for specific platforms).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
* Standard WCAG AA contrast (≥ 4.5:1 for body and labels).
* Support for dark mode switching (`[data-theme="dark"]`).
* Option for reduced motion (disable non-essential animations).
